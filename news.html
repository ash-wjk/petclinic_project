Update on dog fatalities in the Ogdens/Latchmore area

25 November 2016

The Animal Health Trust have produced a questionnaire in conjuction with Anderson Moores Veterinary Specialists and the Forest Veterinary Clinic to help investigate the deaths of dogs in the Fordingbridge area. If you have walked your dog in the New Forest Fordingbridge area since the beginning of 2013, please complete this questionnaire, even if your dog has not been affected.

Case of the week: Justin

20 November 2016

Justin is a 6.5 year old, male neutered, domestic short hair cat. Over the past couple of months he has been drinking and urinating more. He has also seemed to lose weight. He is always hungry and seems otherwise normal. On physical examination his vital signs are normal and appears to be generally otherwise normal on physical examination. Labs showed a mild increase blood glucose at 447 mg/dl. Urinalysis showed a large amount of glucose, small amount of ketones and a large amount of blood.

Animal Fun Day and Dog Show 2016

14 November 2016

The annual Charity Animal Fun Day and Dog Show will be taking place on Sunday 14th August 2016 on the Queens Field next to our West Meads clinic. It's our 7th show and its gets bigger and better each year! This is a great family fun day out and at the same time, raises money for two very worthy animal charities. This year, all proceeds will be going to Guide Dogs and Catastrophes Cat Rescue. Last year we raised a grand total of £2760 and so this year we would very much like to smash that total and raise even more. We can only do this with your support and kind donations.
