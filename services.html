Facilities

We have facilities to carry out a wide range of diagnostic tests from routine skin microscopy to detailed blood analysis. This allows us to obtain rapid results (usually within 2 hours) thereby enabling us to treat your pet more promptly.

Including powerful whole body X-Ray, dedicated dental X-ray, Ultrasound and Endoscopy facilities.

Also, A Full Range Of Surgical Capabilities Each surgery is equipped with modern anaesthetic equipment (we use only the safest gaseous anaesthetic agent, Isoflurane) together with surgical facilities allowing us to perform a wide range of procedures on pets ranging from huge Great Dane dogs down to mice and bats!

Behaviour Counselling

We provide behaviour counselling and treatment for many of the common behaviour conditions found in dogs, cats and rabbits.

Microchip Identification

We provide a complete microchip identification service for almost all species of pet animals from the more usual dogs and cats, to parrots and tortoises.

Professional Dietary Advice

As veterinary surgeons, we have the training and depth of knowledge to provide expert professional advice on how to feed your pet properly to ensure they live a long and healthy life. Whether it be our qualified nurses or our veterinary surgeons, the advice you receive from us will be the best and most up to date.
